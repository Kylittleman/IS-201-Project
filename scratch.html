Why Juice WRLD Was One of the Greatest

Juice WRLD, born Jarad Higgins, was known for his unparalleled freestyle ability, emotional vulnerability in lyrics, and genre-blending sound. He helped pioneer the emo rap wave and left behind a deep impact on millions of fans.

Top 3 Things That Made Juice WRLD Special

  1. Freestyling Ability
    • Once freestyled for over an hour straight on Tim Westwood
    • Could rhyme effortlessly over any beat
  2. Emotional Honesty
    • Talked openly about mental health, heartbreak, and addiction
    • Connected deeply with fans who felt the same
  3. Legacy
    • Over 10B Spotify streams
    • Posthumous albums hit #1 globally
